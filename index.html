Tung Fort

Tung fort (किल्ले तुंग ) is located in Maval taluka of Pune district. The nearest railway station is Malavli which is about 12km away. The fort is located in a picturesque location surrounded by Pavana dam on almost on 3 sides. In my opinion I would suggest you travel by Bikes, cause you’ll only be able to enjoy the surroundings if can feel the breeze over your body. The best season to visit would be monsoon and winter when the area is under lush green colour.

KHANDI Waterfalls

KHANDI Waterfalls Maval is one of the most unexplored places near Pune. It is located to the north of Pune, which is around 80 kms from Pune and 35 kms from Kanhe phata. This place is beautiful and greenery all around, the scenery of the mountains is so delightful with its dense forest. If you are planning for Khandi then you have to take old Pune-Mumbai highway towards Mumbai and then take a turn from Kanhe phata, after travelling for 5 kms you will see one bridge over Indrayani river.

Tikona Fort

Tikona also known as Vitandgad, is the dominant hill fort in Maval in western India. It is located near Kamshet around 60 km from Pune. The village nearest to the fort is called Tikona-Peth. The 3500 foot high hill is pyramidal in shape and the name Tikona means "triangular".

About us

Maval is a Town in Maval Taluka in Pune District of Maharashtra State, India. It belongs to Desh or Paschim Maharashtra region . It belongs to Pune Division . It is located 59 KM towards west from District head quarters Pune. It is a Taluka head quarter. Maval Pin code is 411045 and postal head office is N.I.A.

Maval town is located 59 km west of Pune. Nestled amidst the Sahyadri range, Maval abounds natural and historical marvels with hills, dams, old forts and ancient caves. The word Maval is derived from the Marathi word Maval, meaning the direction in which the sunsets. It is hilly terrain and part of the Sahyadri range/western ghats.
